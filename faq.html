Frequently Asked Questions

What time should I arrive to the ceremony?

Please try to arrive between 4:00pm and 4:30pm to allow for enough time to find your seat. The ceremony will begin no later than 5:00pm.

What is the dress code?

The dress code is semi-formal. Please do not wear shorts or t-shirts.

What will the wedding-day timeline look like?

The ceremony will begin promptly at 5:00pm and will last approximately 30 minutes. Cocktail hour will occur immediately following the ceremony, and the reception will begin around 6:30pm.

How do I get to the wedding venue? Will transportation be provided?

There are a few options for getting to the venue such as driving yourself(there is plenty of on-site parking), Uber/Lyft, and we are looking into shuttle services for those that are staying at the hotel block.

What type of food and drink will be served during the cocktail hour and reception?

The caterer we have chosen is The Peached Tortilla which has amazing southern-asian fusion food. There will also be an open bar for those that are of-age as well as sodas and water.

Will there be an open bar?

Yes there will be an open bar!

Have you booked hotel room blocks for guests?

We are in the process of booking hotel blocks and will provide the information for how to book as soon as we have the details.

Can I take pictures during the ceremony?

The ceremony will be completely unplugged! Please keep your phones away and leave the pictures to the professionals. We will be releasing the pictures as soon as our amazing photographers deliver them to us!

Can I bring my gift to the wedding?

Your presence is present enough, but should you wish to bring a gift, there will be a gift table for your convenience.

When will the reception end?

The reception will end around 11:00pm to allow for time to clean up.
